Palayan City National High School

The 1986 EDSA revolution marked the founding of Palayan City High School during the over threw of Marcos from his throne. Former City Mayor and Congressman Pacifico M. Fajardo was very passionate then with the thought of starting an institution that could serve indigent but deserving students who could not afford to go to private schools and the schools in Cabanatuan City. Palayan City National High School is a school in Nueva Ecija, Central Luzon. Palayan City National High School is situated nearby to the town hall the Palayan City Hall. PCNHS has junior and senior high school year, PCNHS offers education that will provide knowledge that can be carried throughout the life of students through different sections or strands, in Junior high school there are STE (Science Technology and Engineering) SP-ICT (Special Program for Information and Communications Technology), SPS (Special Programs for Sports), and REGULAR SECTION, in Senior high school there are different strands that can be studied which is STEM, ABM, HUMMS, GAS, IT and TVL.
